Entrepreneurs are made, not born

BU Spark! is an initiative to support student driven innovation and entrepreneurship in computer science and computer engineering, and related disciplines.

Find Out More

BU Spark! is an initiative to support student driven innovation in technology. Housed at Boston University’s Hariri Institute for Computing, BU Spark! aims to helps students realize their technical potential by providing access to resources, knowledge, and expert networks to support their product innovation journey.

CREATE A TECHNOVATION




/ BU Spark! Fellowship /

Do you want to pursue a project of your own? If so, BU Spark! Fellowship could help you and your team jumpstart the development of your ideas. The program aims to provide you with a collaborative coworking space, access to mentors, and skill building to help ignite your ideas.

/ BU Innovation Ventures /

Do you want to start your own project, but can't balance with your course load? The CS492 BU Spark! Innovation Ventures class, will walk you through the steps needed to build a technical product in one semester. The program aims to provide you with a collaborative coworking space, access to mentors, and skill building to help ignite your ideas.

PURSUE AN INNOVATION




/ The Spark! X-Lab /

The Spark! X-Lab, or experiential learning program, will provide you the opportunity to work on a real-world project sponsored by a company, start-up, non-profit, or other organization. In Spring 2017, we are offering this opportunity for students enrolled in CS506 Data Science, CS542 Machine Learning, and CE's Android App Development. This will allow you to use work on a Spark! X-Lab project that will also satisfy your class project’s requirements.
